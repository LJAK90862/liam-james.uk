## What you'll learn

This is the complete playbook behind the GTM Orchestra — the 8-agent system I built to automate lead generation, qualification, outreach, and reporting across 10 campaigns.

It's not theory. It's the exact system running right now, sourcing leads, sending emails, and booking meetings while I sleep.

### What's inside

**Part 1 — Architecture**
How to design an agent pipeline where each agent has one job and passes work to the next. Why narrow beats general. How the handoffs actually work.

**Part 2 — Lead Discovery**
How to mine GitHub, Product Hunt, HN, and npm for high-intent leads using fingerprint detection. The search queries, quality filters, and scoring models I use across all 10 campaigns.

**Part 3 — Enrichment & Qualification**
Hunter.io integration for email finding. ICP scoring with Claude. How to separate real products from tutorials, forks, and abandoned repos.

**Part 4 — Outbound Sequences**
The 3-email sequences that get 52% open rates and 4% reply rates. How to write emails that reference the recipient's actual product. What works, what doesn't, and why short beats long.

**Part 5 — Infrastructure**
Smartlead setup, domain warmup strategy, sending limits, inbox rotation. The stuff that determines whether your emails land in the inbox or spam.

**Part 6 — Reporting & Optimisation**
Daily and weekly reporting with Claude analysis. Which metrics matter. How to know when to scale a campaign vs kill it.

**Part 7 — The Full Stack**
Node.js agent scripts, GitHub Actions for scheduling, HubSpot for CRM, Discord for notifications. Everything you need to replicate this yourself.
